Celebrating the Winners

24 October 2023 / Mariana Mallari

In a demonstration of remarkable generosity, Kuya Mayor Channel orchestrated an unforgettable event - the BSCS Grand Meet and Greet at Red Anchor Suites. Twelve fortunate winners are now gearing up for an exclusive experience with complimentary air-conditioned bed spaces and transportation, courtesy of Kuya Mayor Channel. The check-in period is set from October 24 to October 26, 2023, and among the lucky winners are 19 individuals from different corners of the Philippines, all set to enjoy a complimentary 3-day space stay at Red Anchor Suites.


The excitement extends to an invitation to the "Buhay sa Cruise Ship Meet and Greet" on Wednesday, offering winners a chance to connect with like-minded individuals and share maritime experiences. Followers of Kuya Mayor Channel can stay updated with exclusive content from the BSCS Grand Meet and Greet. This event is more than just a celebration; it's a testament to the generosity of Kuya Mayor Channel, fostering camaraderie and providing winners with an unforgettable rendezvous with luxury at Red Anchor Suites.
